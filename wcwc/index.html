Women's World Cup


Introduction

World Cup cricket for women is played in 2 formats. The ODI format is played in 50 overs and is called the Women's Cricket World Cup. The T20I format is played in 20 overs and is termed as the ICC Women's T20 World Cup (or simply Women's T20 World Cup)

There was no final played in the 1973 and 1978 editions of the Women's World Cup. The winner and runner up were decided on points earned throughout the tournament.
